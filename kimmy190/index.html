Browsercast

A simple tool for narrating presentations.

  • Press the play button above to enable audio replay
  • Then click anywhere else on the slide…
  • …and press the down arrow key to advance
  • The audio clips on subsequent slides should play automatically

The Problem

  • Screencasts aren't text: they're pixels in the shape of text
  • So they're not searchable, accessible, linkable, or styleable

The Solution (and Problems)

  • Browsercast plays short snippets of audio as slides progress
  • Uses snap-scroll
  • But:
    • Users must press "play" on the first slide to give permission
    • Each slide's audio must be in a separate file
    • Clips are replayed when slides are revisited
    • Overflow isn't handled gracefully
    • We don't handle captioning or internationalization
